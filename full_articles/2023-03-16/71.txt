![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/7d7ecb5b9ed951f4da31e9b09fb320bc.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)Robert McCracken, Ecosystem Lead at Alchemy Pay, has confirmed that he will attend Global Payment in Web3 and deliver a speech at this themed forum hosted by all-digital public infrastructure PlatON on the afternoon of April 13, 2023.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/e7a1a9330c551b9b0fa5d01f8e19d21b.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)Robert McCracken is now Ecosystem Lead at Alchemy Pay, with 15 years of experience in content development and 4 years of experience in blockchain research. Robert studied blockchain finance under MIT Professor Gary Gensler, the current chair of the SEC.

Founded in Singapore in 2018, Alchemy Pay is a payment gateway that seamlessly connects crypto and global fiat currencies for businesses, developers, and users. The Alchemy Pay Ramp Solution is integrated, via plugin or API, with platforms and dApps, providing an easy onramp from fiat currency to crypto. Alchemy Pay’s supports payments from 173 countries — Visa, Mastercard, Google Pay, Apple Pay, popular regional mobile wallets, and domestic transfers with a focus on emerging markets. Its offramping capability remits to users in 50+ local fiat currencies. ACH is the Alchemy Pay network token on Ethereum and BNB Chain.

Global Payment in Web 3, a major sub-event of the Hong Kong Web3 Festival 2023, has invited big-name guests from around the world to deliver keynote speeches on topics including the development of blockchain payment technology, the trend of borderless payments fostered by Web3, the opportunities and challenges facing cryptocurrency in cross-border payments, regulatory compliance of stablecoins, and how the Web3 gaming industry integrates with payments. Through thought-provoking discussions between guests from different backgrounds such as the banking industry, the gaming industry, acquiring institutions, and issuing institutions, the forum will explore whether new payment standards based on blockchain ledgers and Payment Tokens will transform our definition of “money.”
