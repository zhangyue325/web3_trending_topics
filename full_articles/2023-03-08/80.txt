### EtherPOAP ‘POINT’ Mechanism

The output of ‘POINT’ from OG ‘Proofing’ is based on the three standards: 1.) Rarity of EtherPOAP OG — EtherPOAP OG with purple, green, and golden colors as the main traits would influence the ‘POINT’ output. The less of the relevant color that EtherPOAP OG exists in the NFT collection, the more weight of the ‘POINT’ comes out; 2.) Amount of EtherPOAP OG with ‘Proofing’ — the more amount of EtherPOAP OG been ‘Proofing’, the more weight of ‘POINT’ comes out; 3.) ‘Proofing’ period of EtherPOAP OG — longer time range of OG been in ‘Proofing’ represents more ‘POINT’ comes out.
