![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/d4a25efcbfcb98734af9f1fba14af303.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)3. After logging in, the system will automatically generate a game wallet for you. You can see your wallet information on your Wallets page. If your game wallet is not showing, click the Binding button to try binding again.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/7d29d4c9ba04d9690ed20b0e04e52ac8.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/7809b45816cac3aaa82886775ed7d4dc.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)### Access Metaverse

#### Option 1: Download the CyberPink & WhiteDay Metaverse Client

1. Visit <https://whiteday.ultiverse.io/>
2. Click the “Download” button to download and install the Metaverse Client (Zipped file) on your computer.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/af97c15f4becbbce5b5d77256a411e2d.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)3. Click Val.exe to enter Metaverse.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/a734f35917464a705b5da970b0b810bb.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)#### Option 2: Access the CyberPink & WhiteDay Metaverse through the Cloud Platform.

1. Visit <https://whiteday.ultiverse.io/>
2. Click the “CLOUD GAMING” button to enter the Metaverse via the Cloud Platform. (This is a great option for MAC users, but please note that the Cloud platform is not available on mobile devices.)
3. The Cloud Platform will open at March 14th, 4:00 AM UTC.
![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/c809bc8ba90d46fd7cd558cbf39df108.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)### Sign into your Ultiverse account and select your avatar.

1. When accessing the Metaverse Sign-in page, please enter the email address and password associated with your Ultiverse account.

2. Choose the gender and skin color of your avatar on the bottom right.

3. Click “Sign In.”

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/638814fe33c08b0c2f527fa9a334a9dc.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)4. Enter the WhiteDay Metaverse (The Miracle Vortex), and control your avatar to explore the Metaverse.

A: Move forward

S: Move backward

W: Move right

D: Move left

F: Interact (Collect & Submit heart, enter the lucky draw, etc)

Alt: Show/Hide mouse cursor (When the cursor appears, the mouse will no longer control the character’s direction)

Enter: Open text chat (You can enter messages to talk to other players)

Space: Jump

ESC: Exit Metaverse.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/997be44934ab1e9693e95d240f48455a.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)### Enter Metaverse

1. Upon entering the first floor of Metaverse, your Particle Wallet will automatically connect to your avatar.

2. Move towards the heart in front of your avatar and press “F” to collect it.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/2e889d44070552356515b0676e09f5b9.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)3. Submit the collected heart to the light tree.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/5d55084bd866f87e354205bb38605f70.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)4. The star indicator will appear, follow it to jump to the second floor.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/31cf0be25593373d6c2969703c3c2a90.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/01a07ebc98e7ca9c1ef57381c2b79e20.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)5. You will arrive at the center of the Metaverse. (the Third Day Sakura.)

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/2ee849409aa13ae987b452505300cb94.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)### The Rules of the CyberPink & WhiteDay Event

Take part in the daily firework show with the chance to win generous USDT prizes! To participate, complete a specified number of tasks each day and hold a badge (NFT). These NFTs can be minted in-game or on the Ultiverse Mission Runner, with a different badge offered each day. (https://mission.ultiverse.io/project/whiteday)

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/869762791dddaacd4dc161a19d0a6da1.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)Each account is limited to two lucky draws per day. To be eligible, you must submit 15 hearts per day and hold a badge (NFT). The first 15 hearts can be obtained by completing a quiz after watching a video (only once), then directly obtained by triggering the sponsor’s booth.

Eight drawings occur every day within the fireworks, spaced four hours apart, tentatively scheduled for (UTC):

Morning: 4:30 AM, 5:00 AM, 5:30 AM, 6:00 AM

Afternoon: 4:30 PM, 5:00 PM, 5:30 PM, 6:00 PM

Note that USDT is the only prize distributed in the fireworks lucky draw.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/ab2a47068f3d52c839b99e6042b7d20a.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)### Start your event journey

1. The tasks are located in three areas. To collect a hearts, approach a booths, watch the videos, and answer the questions correctly.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/28031857c66b58afb31e4b676de64fb9.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/62abafb2460af18c73edacdd28e9c32d.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/4bf7a7ac267227820383b26b89f66f9a.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/04aba7b5140712b2e204dc1393af579a.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/b43fb7b8c995a5592129938732f84602.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)2. After collecting all the hearts from each booth, submit them to the task tree located in the center of each area to complete that area. Repeat these steps in each of the three areas to finish all the tasks.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/f60d086541fb827c60aca9ff34d6de3e.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)### Enjoy the firework shows and enter the lucky draw

1. Keep an eye on the countdown for each firework show. The firework display will start when the countdown reaches 5 minutes remaining, and the lucky draw box will appear when the countdown ends.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/f0d30aa565163ca3da3ec3522fab6bae.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)2. You can enter the lucky draw during the firework show (two times per day).

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/41c3b4142a1837cfb4953e65ecf23b0b.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)3. To participate in the Fireworks lucky draw, you need to complete a designated number of tasks daily and possess a badge (NFT). These badges can be acquired either in-game or through the Ultiverse Mission Runner platform, with a new badge available each day.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/4f722776f621f2dd382161268ebeaddb.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/f291fab453af499005b955a889686032.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/8cf3fc7e74ca657a9aedf31dba860d52.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)4. Verify your draw status.

5. If you don’t win on the first try, wait for the second opportunity during the next firework show.

6. If you’re a winner, claim your reward by submitting verification in your Particle Wallet.

7. Your reward will be sent to your Particle Wallet.

![](https://img.foresightnews.pro/202303/cee619713feb93acf4db7a4de7eec8b3.png?x-oss-process=style/scale70)8. After completing all tasks, collect 15 hearts the following day within the circle in front of each sponsor’s booth. This makes you eligible to participate in the fireworks lucky draw. (Note: Without collecting all 15 hearts, you can not participate in the fireworks lucky draw.) If you have logged out, you will need to recollect all the hearts in order to participate in the firework lucky draw.
